Town Hall Bakery and Dail Special Cafe will open for the season June 26th 2021. Greatful to be back for our 30th season bringing you delicious baked goods made from scratch!

Established in 1991 by proprietors Krista Olson and Don Jervis, the Town Hall Bakery and Daily Special Cafe is located in the historic Nugent and Kroc building in Jacksonport, WI. Family owned and operated; always bringing you fresh, from-scratch baked goods and other delights. Open every weekend with a selection of treats, coffee, picnic items, and more TO-GO. Come see us on the quiet side.
