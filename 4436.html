Our chefs artfully combine the freshest ingredients to create cusine of superior quality.

The Town Hall Bakery has been serving delicious food for over 23 years. Owned an operated by Krista Olson and Don Jervis, the Town Hall Bakery caters parties, serves breakfast and lunch, bakes beautifully decorated cakes, and provides music, art, and theatre to the local community. We are famous for our cherry pies, caramel rolls, skorpa, scones, cherry oatmeal cookies, granola, and cakes. We are localed in a historic building and are ecologically conscious.

The Town Hall Bakery was built in 1890, and is a historic landmark in Jacksonport.
